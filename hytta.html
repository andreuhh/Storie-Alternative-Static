Hytta

October 2020

Html, css, js, WooCommerce

Description

I made this project as the final project of the Mohole School course with the hope that one day it will become reality. Hytta is an online platform for booking outdoor activities and renting sports equipment.



What sets it apart from the competition is that each user can rent their own equipment to other members of the Community.



Benchmark

First I did a direct Benchmark to analyze the strengths and weaknesses of the competitors. I discovered that only ShareWood offers the possibility of private rental. I have marked the strengths and weaknesses of each site on a table.

Target e Personas

As a main target I identified people between 24 and 37 years (mostly males). They are active people, they love to play sports and be outdoors.


Then I focused on the personas, assuming an ideal scenario for a rental situation.

Sitemap

The next step of my process was to develop the navigation system by organizing the site map in a clear and simple layout. I created the overall structure of the website based on the information I gathered in the previous steps.

Brand Identity

La Hytta is the small house in the woods far from the city where Norwegians usually spend their weekends. I decided to use this name because it is short and easy to remember and because it refers to contact with nature.
Starting from the name I created the logo.

Color Palette

Wireframing

Based on the feedback and the insights I learned from the sketching phase, I began to design my first wireframes using Figma. I made sure to prioritise the features that would best address the needs of the users and the business goals throughout the website.

Prototype

After carrying out some user tests on the wireframe, I built the site prototype starting from the single components up to the more complex structures (Atomic Design).
Later I did other user tests using the high fidelity prototype.

Development

I developed the site by creating a custom WordPress theme. Later I used WooCommerce for the management of activities and rentals.
