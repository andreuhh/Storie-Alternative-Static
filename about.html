I am a frontend developer who loves photography and sport.

The high school aptitude test said I was going to be an agent secret, a kind of James Bond but lower. I honestly never understood if that test was true or not but I never liked lies and elegant clothes, so I chose a different path.


I did various jobs before devoting myself day and night to web development and interface creation and in this period I discovered 3 things about myself: I love code and its mysterious side, I love solving problems and I love people.

In my life, sports and travel play a very important role. Traveling with my camera helped me discover the beauty of this world and respect for cultures other than mine.


Running helps me in times of stress, it allows me to set and reach new goals and I often find the solution to a problem during the first 3 kilometers of running in a sort of brainstorming in motion.
